Who am I


I am Enindu Alahapperuma, a back-end developer from Sri Lanka. I do back-end programming, web development and a little bit of logo designing. As a web developer, I know HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I do not design or develop front-end. For the record, I do not like front-end and JavaScript. I only concerned about UX, not UI.


My core strength is back-end. I do back-end programming with PHP, Go, Node.js and Deno. I do not like Node.js much, because of JavaScript, but I must admit that, it is a good library after all. Because of that love-hate relationship, I choose Deno over Node.js, a decent library with full TypeScript support, out-of-the-box. On the other hand, PHP is the most hated language on the planet. Here I am, still using PHP. I mostly use PHP for enterprise-level web applications. It is solid as a rock and matured like a fine whiskey bottle. If you know how to write PHP, you know PHP. If you are concerned, I know Laravel, but I do not like it. And last, not least, Go, a strict-typed, compiled, functional, general-purpose programming language from Google, is the most elegant language across the all languages I use. I just love it.


For the record, I do not know C or C++ or C# or Java or Python or Ruby or any other programming language as most people do.


I like simplicity. (I use Arch, by the way) Simplicity is not what most people think of. Simplicity is the low-level. I do not like to be bloated. I know, what I know. I will know, what I want to know. I do not know everything.


I use Linux and do not know Windows or MacOS or BSD. For the record, I use open source software only. I also do a little bit of logo designing. For that i use Inkscape mostly. And I also know GIMP, a little bit. But, I am not a graphic designer.

What is This Website


This is my personal website. I post articles and works here. But this is not a portfolio website that, I share my works with the public.


There is a story behind this website. My name is kinda unique one in Sri Lanka. As far as I know, I am the first person, who named after this name. I recently found these two people with my name and I am very concerned about it. So I decided to take this domain. That is the true reason behind this website. It is not about sharing articles or my works with the public in the first place.


After all, here I am writing articles and sharing works.


This entire website is written in Go and compiled into vanilla HTML and CSS. There is no JavaScript magic here. I was too lazy to write HTML codes, so I created this static page generator (Kinda). I did not used any external libraries to create this. Everything depends on standard Go libraries.


Since the content of these pages are rendered across the entire screen, there is an accessibility issue, for sure. Who cares. Accessibility is for dummies.
